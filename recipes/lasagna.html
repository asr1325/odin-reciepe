Lasagna

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical

lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
